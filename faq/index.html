Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our free face to emoji tool at getfacetoemoji.com.

What is face to emoji?
Face to emoji means replacing faces in a photo with emoji stickers. GetFaceToEmoji detects each face automatically and places a matching emoji. You can fine-tune size, position, and opacity in Edit mode.
Is GetFaceToEmoji free?
Yes. GetFaceToEmoji is a free online face to emoji converter. No account, subscription, or app install is required.
Are my photos uploaded to a server?
No. All processing runs locally in your browser. Your images are not sent to our servers for face detection or emoji editing.
How do I convert a face to emoji?
Open the home page, upload or drop your image, tap Auto for detection, then Download Edited Image. See How it works for a step-by-step guide.
Can I blur faces instead of using emoji?
Yes. Select Blur Circle in the emoji menu to cover a face with a blur sticker. Use Edit mode to move or resize it like any other sticker.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. GetFaceToEmoji works in modern mobile browsers. Upload from your camera roll, run Auto, and download the result—no app store install needed.
