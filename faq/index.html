Common questions

Face to emoji, privacy, and using getfacetoemoji.com.

What is face to emoji?

Replacing faces in a photo with emoji stickers—detected and placed automatically, with optional edits.

Is it free?

Yes. No account, subscription, or app install.

Are photos uploaded?

No. Processing runs in your browser; images stay on your device.

How do I start?

Upload a photo, tap Auto, then download. See How it works.

Blur instead of emoji?

Choose Blur Circle in the emoji menu, then adjust in Edit mode.

Mobile?

Works in modern mobile browsers—upload, Auto, download. No app store needed.
