How to convert face to emoji

GetFaceToEmoji is a free face to emoji tool that runs entirely in your browser at getfacetoemoji.com. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload your photo — On the home page, drop an image into Live Preview or click to choose a file from your device.
  2. Run Auto detection — Tap Auto. The tool detects faces and places emoji stickers. AI models load on first use (only when needed).
  3. Edit (optional) — Turn on Edit to move or resize stickers, pick a different emoji, use Blur Circle, or drag on empty space to add a new sticker.
  4. Download — Tap Download Edited Image to save your face to emoji result as a PNG.

Tips

  • Click Face to Emoji at the top of the home page to reset and start over.
  • Your photos never leave your device—processing stays in the browser.
