How it works

Four steps. Private, in-browser editing at getfacetoemoji.com.

  1. 1

    Upload

    Drop a photo in Live Preview or click to browse.

  2. 2

    Auto

    Tap Auto to detect faces and place emojis. Models load on first use only.

  3. 3

    Edit

    Optional: move stickers, change emoji, add blur, or drag to add new ones.

  4. 4

    Download

    Save your image as PNG with Download Edited Image.

Tips

  • Tap Face to Emoji on the home page to reset.
  • Photos stay on your device—nothing is uploaded.
